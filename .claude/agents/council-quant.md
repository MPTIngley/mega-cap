- Are institutional flows positive or negative?

1. **Data Summary** (2-3 sentences): What the numbers say
2. **Key Metrics**: Specific numbers with context (RSI, P/E, etc.)
3. **Technical Levels**: Support, resistance, and key MAs
4. **Recommendation**: bullish / bearish / neutral
5. **Confidence**: 0-100

Keep response under 300 words. Cite specific numbers. No hand-waving.
